This is a short compilation of my work focused on music.

The Thing

"The Thing" is an Ableton wavetable instrument I made for music, mainly targeted at Lo-fi and Ambient music. It works for other genres as well, due to 8* macros that enables you to change the sound to your liking, from a pad to a pluck and vise versa.

Grainz

"Grainz" is a sample pack made of pure low end focused clean pads for Ambient and more EDM focused music. The samples are made delicately with Quanta and Pigments, using samples and foleys I have recorded over the years of my music production.

RC 20 At Home

RC 20 At Home is a simple Ableton effects rack based on the RC 20 plugin. It has most parameters from RC 20 but the sound is not fully accurate, because of stock effect limitations.

EDM Essentials for Serum

A preset pack created for serum with sounds from the new age of EDM and more.
