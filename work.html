This is a short compilation of my work focused on music and 3D.

The Thing

"The Thing" is a wavetable instrument I made for music, mainly targeted at Lo-fi and Ambient music. It works for other genres as well, due to 8* macros that enables you to change the sound to your liking, from a pad to a pluck and vise versa.

Grainz

"Grainz" is a sample pack made of pure low end focused clean pads for Ambient and more EDM focused music. The samples are made delicately with Quanta and Pigments, using samples and foleys I have recorded over the years of my music production.

Drums that never knock

"DTNK" is a drum kit I have made over a short span of 2 years. It is still in its WIP phase. The drum sounds are made from KICK 2 by CableGuys and other layered foleys.

EDM Essentials for Serum

A preset pack created for serum with sounds from the new age of EDM and more.
